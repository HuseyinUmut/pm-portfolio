The Story Behind the Strategy

Hi, I'm Alexandra Mercer — a product manager who believes great products are built at the intersection of human empathy and sharp business instinct. I've worked across fintech, SaaS, and consumer tech, leading cross-functional teams of designers, engineers, and analysts.

My approach is rooted in continuous discovery. I live in user research, A/B tests, and roadmap trade-offs. But above all, I care about one thing: does this create real value for real people?

When I'm not shipping products, you'll find me mentoring junior PMs, speaking at ProductCon, or hiking somewhere with too much elevation gain.
